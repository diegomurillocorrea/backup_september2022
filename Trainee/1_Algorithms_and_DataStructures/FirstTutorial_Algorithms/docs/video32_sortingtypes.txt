COMPARISON SORTS

Naive Sorts:
Keep looking and comparing values
until the list is sorted
• Bubble Sort
• Insertion Sort
• Selection Sort

Divide and Conquer Sorts
Recursively divide lists and sort 
smaller parts of list until entire
list is sorted
• Mergesort
• Quiksort

Bubble Sort:
Loop through an array, comparing adjacent
indices and swapping the greater value
to the end

Merge Sort:
Recursively merge sorted sub-lists

Mergin Lists:
The merge step takes two sorted lists 
and merges them into 1 sorted list
